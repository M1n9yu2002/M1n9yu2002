## Selected Projects

### Customer Behavior–Driven Risk Analysis

A platform risk analytics case study that converts transaction-level trading behaviour into interpretable customer-level risk indicators. The project focuses on high-exposure trades, FOMO-like buying behaviour, market-order reliance, low-balance reloading behaviour, and targeted risk intervention.

Repository: [`risk-management-customer-behavior-analysis`](https://github.com/M1n9yu2002/risk-management-customer-behavior-analysis)

---

### Robust Optimisation and Predictive Modelling Pipeline

An applied statistical modelling portfolio combining robust optimisation and predictive modelling. The project evaluates Differential Evolution configurations under uncertainty and builds an end-to-end predictive modelling workflow covering exploratory data analysis, validation, and holdout evaluation.

Repository: [`robust-optimisation-and-predictive-pipeline`](https://github.com/M1n9yu2002/robust-optimisation-and-predictive-pipeline)

---

### MindPass FinTech Escrow DApp

A hybrid FinTech DApp prototype demonstrating programmable escrow, subsidy-aware funding, and verifiable settlement for appointment-based digital services. The project combines Next.js, Solidity, Hardhat, Wagmi/viem, and Supabase.

Repository: [`mindpass-fintech-escrow-dapp`](https://github.com/M1n9yu2002/mindpass-fintech-escrow-dapp)

---

### Market Simulation Experiments

Agent-based market simulation experiments exploring equilibrium dynamics, market shocks, and market behaviour under controlled assumptions.

Repository: [`market-simulation-experiments`](https://github.com/M1n9yu2002/market-simulation-experiments)
